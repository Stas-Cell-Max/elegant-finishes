Design

Through meticulous analysis of your requests and space, we evaluate dimensions, finishes, lines, and shapes to ensure solutions that perfectly match your personality and lifestyle. We transform your ideas and desires into personalized, tailor-made creations, expertly crafting each detail to reflect the unique character of your home.

Materials Selection

Placing orders and communicating with vendors can be a tedious and time consuming process. We take that burden off your shoulders. We’ve built relationships with vendors and are happy to order and expedite all items needed for your project

Removal

We handle the removal and demolition of existing installations with precision and care. Our team ensures that this necessary first step is done safely and efficiently, preparing your space for its transformation. We manage all aspects of the teardown process, minimizing disruption and maintaining a clean work environment throughout.

Installation

Our installation service is all about precision and attention to detail. We expertly install trim, flooring, cabinetry, and more, ensuring everything fits perfectly and functions seamlessly. Our skilled team works efficiently, keeping your project on schedule and ensuring a clean finish that enhances your space.

Repairs

Our repair services focus on restoring the functionality and appearance of your carpentry installations. Whether it's fixing wear and tear on cabinetry, mending damaged trim, or correcting flooring issues, we apply our expertise to bring your features back to their best condition. We ensure reliable and lasting repairs, with minimal disruption to your daily life.
