Who I am

Phone Consultation... where the first step is taken

Scheduling a phone consultation enables us to gain a deeper understanding of your remodeling needs and preferences. The more details you provide, the better we can grasp your vision and help bring it to life.
Phone Call

In Home Consultation... discovery meeting in person.

After determining your needs and if the services of Maxidom meet your requirements, we then schedule an appointment at your location and typically take about 30 minutes. It gives us a chance to discuss your dream design and requirements while we assess and measure your physical space.
On Site Visit

Quote Delivery... where the first step is taken

Within few days, we present a preliminary budget for what can be achieved within the amount you are willing to invest in your renovation.
Quote Delivery

Quote Approval & Agreement... where the first step is taken

When the quotation meets the your budget and timeline, the quotation will be converted to an agreement which highlights scope of work, payment breakdown and Maxidom policies.
Agreement

Project Execution/Deposit Collection... when the renovation dream begins.

The remodeling project is scheduled, a deposit is collected and the path to your dream renovation begins!
